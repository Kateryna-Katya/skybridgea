When the Page Comes Alive

It's totally random, so you never know what's coming next.
Sometimes the book just goes off the rails - random things happen that completely change everything.:

  • Mixing

    Symbols on the field rearrange, creating new patterns and possibilities.

  • Sticky Line

  • Miracle Reflection

A special symbol can activate a re-spin state, allowing the current setup to continue and evolve rather than reset immediately.
This creates moments where anticipation builds gradually, instead of resolving all at once.
